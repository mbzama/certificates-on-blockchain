Enrolment

University will award certificates by following two step verification process (College and Student).  
College is responsible for verifying if student was enrolled and completed the education without any issues. This eradicates the fraud issuance of certificates.
Student is responsible to validate his personal details (Name, Date of Birth, Branch, College etc.,) to avoid correction and re-printing of certificates.
